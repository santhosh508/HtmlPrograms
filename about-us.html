About 420 Papad

We are Agrawal Papad Pvt. Ltd. India's leading manufacturers in Papads and Instant Mixeses. We are known for High quality and impeccable taste since 1962.

Who We Are

We have a presence in the field of Papad and Instant Mixeses and are central India’s leading brand since 1962

We are one of India’s leading papad manufacturers.Our speciality lies in the manufacturing of 6 different papad products like Moong Dal Papad, Chana Dal Papad, Urad Dal Papad and more.

With two manufacturing units in Indore, we hold a strong presence across different states in India. Infact, due to the mouthwatering taste of our products, we have to inevitably fill orders from Dubai, New Zealand, United States, Japan, Australia and UAE

Our papads are known for their imperishable taste and superior quality,along with the range of the Instant Mixeses like Khaman mix, Gulabjamun mix, moong bhajiya mix, Idli mix, Dosa mix, we are ceaselessly striving towards our new endeavours.

We have a commercial representation in large retail stores, which itself is a testimonial to our superior taste that has put us ahead of our competitors.

History

Agrawal Papad Pvt. Ltd. started its journey five decades ago and today it leads the market in papad manufacturing.

The company was anchored by Mr. Hukum Chand Agrawal in 1962 with 150 rupees as a starting finance, 5 kgs moong dal and a strong desire to sell good quality papad and so the enterprise which began from a small venture grew into a “Big Name”.

Shakespeare rightly said,” What’s in the name?”.

The unusual sounding name ‘420’ was given by its pioneers, inspired by the legendary Raj Kapoor’s movie ‘Shree 420’. Mr. Hukumchand summed up the meaning of the name stating that hard work paves way for success, no matter what the name is and today the name stands for its irony- ‘TRUST’ only because of its quality assurance & its mouthwatering taste.

‘420’ as known by people otherwise stands for a crook or fraud, but our hard work and consistent quality output changed people’s outlook towards the word. ’420’ today implies hard work and authenticity, in the world of food lovers. The huge business empire saw its expansion in the guidance of the entire family, well woven in the network of all cousins.

“The journey began with ‘Papad’, serving the needs of the mass craving for ‘The Dadi Maa Taste.’ The adds-ons such as various Instant Mixeses etc joined the wagon later due to the needs of products which are non-seasonal and can be produced round the year. The business has expanded its wings in many states like Madhya Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Delhi, Gujarat, Rajasthan, Karnataka, Haryana , Bihar, Punjab, Maharashtra, Andhra Pradesh, Uttar Pradesh, Jharkhand, Tamilnadu, Uttarakhand, Assam, Orissa, West Bengal, and even overseas Like New Zealand, USA, UAE & AUSTRALIA.”

All through the five decades of manufacturing prowess, Agrawal’s 420 papad have a single motto, i.e. “If you have a superior quality and sense of pride in what you produce, your customers are going to keep coming back to you, no matter what so ever your name is”.

With our decades of experience and expertise, we are proud of our ever-growing standards of quality and customer satisfaction.

Quality Policy

As the demand for our Papad increased manifold along with increased capacity, we incorporated modern methodologies of quality control and hygienic plant management.

Directors of the group with more than 20 years of experience in the field, directly handle the quality control department. The basic raw materials come from 420 Papad’s own flour grinding mill, so that the company can exert total control over product quality. The pulses, grains and spices are sourced from Western India's best markets in peak seasons with pre-defined specifications and desired storage conditions (kept in scientific storage).

The automated packaging unit ensures long-lasting freshness & total hygiene of the products.
